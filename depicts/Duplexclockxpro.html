Compatible with iOS 11~11.3.1(Tested on iX Only)

Requires iPhone X, or a tweak that enables the iPhone X status bar


This Tweak allows you to customise Clock for 3+CC different timezone in your Iphone X Statusbar(Right,Left(2),Control Center).

Modify Your Clock with huge preference settings for statusbar of your X.

Go to http://www.nsdateformatter.com/ for more date time format settings.

Google "http://iosfonts.com/" to find fonts.Try-"HelveticaNeue-Bold,Noteworthy-bold,Chalkduster".

A Hugely updated version of DuplexClock,Undertime by sharedRoutine,gilshahar7,NeinZedd9,MPG13 forked partially by manu.

Note:Prefrence bug fixed but still buggy.Change the color setting and respring.(Tweak Enable on/off & 1st respring & uicache if color not update)
